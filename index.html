Eline Visser

I'm a postdoctoral researcher (Wenner-Gren Fellow, funded by the Wenner-Gren Foundations) hosted by the University of Oslo. I work on clausal organisation in Uruangnirin, an Austronesian language of eastern Indonesia. I'm related to the project group Where does grammar come from?

I'm also a member of the Serial Verb Constructions across modalities research group at the University of Amsterdam.

Previously, I worked on Scandinavian prosody in the ExSynOp project at UiT The Arctic University of Norway.

My PhD is from Lund University (Sweden), for which I wrote a grammar of Kalamang, a Papuan language of eastern Indonesia and a neighbour language of Uruangnirin. I also made a dictionary and an archive.
